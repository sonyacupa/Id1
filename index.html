Introduction to Interactive Design 1: What I hope to accomplish in class

What I hope to accomplish in Interactive Design 1 is the ability to understand HTML and CSS basic principles, able to read source codes online, critically think and critique web work. My goal for the end of class is to:

  • Know 15 CSS style names and functions by heart
  • Know 15 HTML tags names and functions by heart
  • Create a randomize recipe generator pulled from Pinterest
  • Create a web and mobile for previous project made foor print

About Me - Tell me about your path to becoming a designer.

My journey as a web designer just began right after Martin Luther King Jr. Day in January. I don't have web experience even a complete web design degree, but I do have skills that got to where I am today.

For four years, I was a multimedia journalist. I uncovered in depth stories of the average joe with photography and film. I love getting to know someone. I was not in love with breaking news. I wanted to fix people I cared, but I could only be an observer.

I left my job and was in search for something new. I put away my camera for almost a year and served coffee as a barista. Everyone thought I was crazy, but I enjoyed creating drinks in it's simple form. I love writing down the codes and remembering what is in it. I didn't know I was learning to design on the spot.

I went back to school and started taking classes again. It was instant love. All the research, the designing made me feel like I'm working for the greater good. I didn't feel like I was observing, but helping. Ultimately, I envision myself to be a UX designer.

I want to reconstruct something that is broken. I want to research to making something better. I want to be someone who can be there and connect with them in someway.

know relearning a new skill is scary, but I know that if your dreams don't scare you, you're not dreaming big enough. Oh it scares me, and I can't wait to get into the thick of it.

About Me - Tell me what you hope to accomplish with design

What I hope to accomplish with design is design with a purpose. I've always been into fixing something or making something better. I hope to work on social justice, immigration and women empowerment.
